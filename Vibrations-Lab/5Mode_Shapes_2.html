Visualisation Guidance

This is the guidance for the Experimental Mode Shapes visualisation.

×

You can import the experimental data in the three input boxes above. As it is constant, the resonance frequency is loaded only once, at the beginning. The Amplitudes and Phase Angles are loaded one by one. To record the data, press "Submit".

×

Anything that you input after the data for the ninth accelerometer will not be recorded. To start over press "Reset".

×

The "Match Frequencies" and "Unmatch Frequencies" toggle button allows you to set the frequencies of the theoretical mode shape and your experimental data to be the same. This allows for a better comparison of the eigenvectors.

×

Click on the "Start" button to see the mode shape animate. To stop the animation just click on the "Stop" button.

×

The "Show previous/new experimental data" button allows you to see previous experimental data and compare it to yours

×

Click on the "Show theory" button to see the underlying theory behind this visualisation

×

Have fun!
