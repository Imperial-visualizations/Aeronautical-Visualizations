Visualisation Guidance

This is the guidance for the Experimental Mode Shapes visualisation.

×

Here you can import the experimental data. As it is constant, the resonance frequency is loaded only once, at the beginning. The Amplitudes and Phase Angles are loaded one by one. To record the data, press "Submit".

×

Once you have recorded the data for all nine accelerometers, anything that you input after that will not be recorded. To start over press "Reset".

×

The "Match Frequencies" and "Unmatch Frequencies" toggle button allows you to set the frequencies of the theoretical mode shape with your experimental values to be the same. This allows for a better comparison of the eigenvectors.

×

Click on the "Start" button to see the mode shape animate. To stop the animation just click on the "Stop" button.

×

Click on the "Show theory" button to see the underlying theory behind this visualisation

×

Have fun!
