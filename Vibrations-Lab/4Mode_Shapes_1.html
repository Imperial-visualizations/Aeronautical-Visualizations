Visualisation Guidance

This is the guidance for the Mode Shapes visualisation.

×

Using the drop-down list above you can choose the mode shape of the structure that you want to see.

×

Click on the "Start" button above to see the mode shape animate. To stop the animation just click on the "Stop" button.

×

Click on the "Show theory" button on the left to see the underlying theory behind this visualisation.

×

The slider above allows you to slow down the animation (useful especially for higher mode shapes). Note that the animation is already in slow motion so that it can be viewed properly.

×

The left and right blue arrows allow you to move between the slides. Clicking the right one will take you to the 3D simulation of the mode shapes

×
