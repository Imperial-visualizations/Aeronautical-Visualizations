Visualisation Guidance

This is the guidance for the Mode Shapes visualisation.

×

Here you can choose the mode shape of the structure that you want to see. There are nine of them.

×

Click on the "Start" button to see the mode shape animate. To stop the animation just click on the "Stop" button.

×

Click on the "Show theory" button to see the underlying theory behind this visualisation

×

The left and right blue arrows allow you to move between the slides. Clicking the right one will take you to the 3D simulation of the mode shapes

×

Have fun!
