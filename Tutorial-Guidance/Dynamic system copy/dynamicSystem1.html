Equations of motion

Force equilibrium of mass $m$ leads to: $$ m\ddot{r} + kr - k\ell\theta = R $$

Moment equilibrium of the rod around its bottom pin leads to: $$ I\ddot{\theta} + (k_\theta + k\ell^2)\theta - k\ell r = \Theta $$

In matrix form: $$\begin{bmatrix}m & 0\\0 & I\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix} \ddot{r} \\ \ddot{θ} \end{bmatrix} + \begin{bmatrix}k & -k\ell\\-k\ell & k_θ+k\ell^2\end{bmatrix} \begin{bmatrix}r\\θ\end{bmatrix} = \begin{bmatrix}R\\Θ\end{bmatrix} $$
